About SKY UNI

With the Covid epidemic still showing no signs of stopping, and working online remotely becoming a trend, the development of applications serving those needs is growing frantically.

We, as the people who have been experiencing such services, especially at extremely impactful aspects such as online learning, faced a lot of real world problems every single seconds, but no one has yet to be solved, causing tons of annoyances for everyone and leading to a severe loss of efficiency.

Therefore, we have desperately decided, we will be the pioneer, ahead of our time, and thoroughly solve those problems, to bring to the world a global revolution. And that is the mission of SKY UNI.

Improve the quality of Online Classes

SKY UNI offers a continuous roll attendance by checking student attendance at all times

Applied AI technology for facial recognition, SKY UNI allows real-time online attendance. It also records all the study time parameters of all students.

Automatically detects distractions and allows sending individual alerts to every learner.

Traditional library Digitization

Provides online storage for all resources

Suggest suitable textbooks and materials that are personalized for each student, including appropriate factors for their career orientation.

Fostering out-standing students and supporting weak students.

Based on each individual's needs and statistics to build a personalized learning roadmap.

Automate the process of Parking

Easily access real-time vehicle location, minimizing the possibility of vehicle loss

Minimize the cost of human resources for parking management

Save time swiping the card when the camera can be easily identified through the license plate

Quickly detect and easily retrieve timeframes when something goes wrong

Gigantic Job Opportunities

Students will be automatically suggested recruitment news that is highly relevant to their orientation, abilities, and aspirations.

Sustainable Recruitment Network

With data collected from student information, SKY UNI offers a personalized connection solution. With that information source, the system outlines everything into a virtual CV for each individual, focusing on highlight keywords suitable to each profession and student's career aspirations - which will be focused on by HR.

HR will love to know that the system will also automatically match the candidate's information and propose a list of the most suitable resumes.

Free of Charge

Completely free for all educational partners.

Development Roadmap

Technologies research

In the early phase of the project, we focus on technology research, market analysis, project feasibility assessment. In addition, we also aim to complete the demo for market research and call for cooperation.

Project Integration

Currently, we have called for partnerships from educational providers like MindX and Iliat. Our goal at this stage is to integrate against existing platforms, in order to minimize costs of development and pilot implementation. At the same time, at this stage, we will go deeper into Business analysis, user needs assessment and feature improvement.

Scale Up

After stabilizing operations in the pilot units, we conduct Scale Up projects to capture market share. It is expected that in the first 6 months of 2022, we will cooperate with at least 2 more educational units, and 4 more in the last 6 months of the year. Estimated business growth of 250%.

Globalization

Deploying a global model, focusing on upgrading infrastructure for large traffic, the immediate target is Southeast Asia. In addition, it is necessary to focus on changes to suit the needs of each customer partner unit.
